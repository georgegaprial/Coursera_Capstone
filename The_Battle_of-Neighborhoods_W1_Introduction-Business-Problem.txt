Sally Cafe Shope in "Bogotá, Colombia"
______________________________________

Introduction
-------------
Sally wants to open her Cafe in some Locality (District)
in Bogotá, Colombia.
And she is asking our help in finding the best Locality.


Business Problem 
----------------
In order to open Sally's Cafe, we will need 
to use some information about the Localities in Bogotá, Colombia.

We must be sure that the place where the cafe is opened
will have enough customers and also that there aren't a large ammount
of cafes in the place.
