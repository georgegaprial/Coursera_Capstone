Sally Burger Restaurant in New York City
________________________________________

Introduction
-------------
Sally wants to open her Burger Joint in some Locality (District)
in New York City.
And she is asking our help in finding the best Locality.


Business Problem 
----------------
In order to open Sally's burger joint, we will need 
to use some information about the Localities in New York City.

We must be sure that the place where the burger joint is opened
will have enough customers and also that there aren't a large ammount
of burer joints in the place.
